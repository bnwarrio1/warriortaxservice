FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tax preparation services.

When does tax season start?

Tax season typically begins in January when the IRS starts accepting e-filed returns. You can gather your documents early, but returns are not filed until all official tax documents (W-2s, 1099s) are received.

Do I need my W-2 to file my taxes?

Yes. We do not file tax returns without official W-2s and other required income documents. This protects you from errors, delays, and potential IRS issues.

What is a refund advance?

A refund advance is a loan based on your expected tax refund, allowing you to receive funds before the IRS processes your return. Advances are subject to approval by our third-party financial partners and are not guaranteed.

What if I made a mistake on a previous return?

We can help you file an amended return (Form 1040-X) to correct errors on previously filed returns. It's important to address mistakes as soon as possible to avoid penalties.

Do you offer IRS audit assistance?

Yes. If you receive an IRS notice or are selected for audit, we provide assistance to help you navigate the process and protect your rights. Our team will work with you every step of the way.

Can you file my taxes if I live outside Chicago?

Absolutely. We serve clients in all 50 states. We can work with you remotely — you can send us your documents electronically and we'll prepare your return without needing an in-person visit.
